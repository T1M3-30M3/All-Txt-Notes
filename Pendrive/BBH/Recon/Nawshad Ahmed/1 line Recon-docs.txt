🔍 Bug Bounty Recon Tip: 5 One-Liner Commands for Effective Reconnaissance

1️⃣ Google Dorks for Recon:

site:*.google.*
site:google.*
site:*.google[.]com
site:*.google.-*.*

site:groups[.]google[.]com "example[.]com"

Find juicy Endpoints for further testing 🎯

Great for uncovering specific Domain-related vulnerabilities.

2️⃣ Extract JS Files with Katana:

- katana -u https://test[.]com -jc -d 2 | grep ".js$" | uniq | sort > js.txt

Ideal for discovering JavaScript files on a target domain.


3️⃣ Fuzz Finding Hidden Directories:

/.FUZZ
/-FUZZ
/~FUZZ
/../FuZZ

Helps in locating hidden directories and files.


4️⃣ Search for Login Pages:
inurl:login | inurl:signin | intitle:login | intitle:signin | inurl:secure site:test[.]com

Useful for identifying login portals and potentially vulnerable endpoints.


5️⃣ Find Emails:
grep -E -o "\b[A-Za-z0-9._%+-]+@[A-Za-z0-9.-]+\.[A-Za-z]{2,}\b" file.txt

Extracts email addresses from text files, aiding in contact discovery.
